BATMAN


BATMAN OPERATES IN THE FICTIONAL GOTHAM CITY WITH ASSISTANCE FROM VARIOUS SUPPORTING CHARACTERS ,INCLUDING HIS BUTLER ALFRED,

POLICE COMMISSIONER JIM GORDON , AND VIGILANTE ALLIES SUCH AS ROBIN.UNLIKE MOST SUPERHEROES,BATMAN DOES NOT POSSESS ANY INHUMAN SUPERPOWERS.
